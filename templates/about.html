About Us

Welcome to Transcripto! Our mission is to make audio transcription accessible and efficient for everyone. With our innovative application, users can easily upload videos, and our advanced Whisper model will transcribe the audio into text, complete with speaker labels and timestamps.

Whether you're a student, professional, or just someone who needs to convert audio to text, Transcripto is designed with you in mind. We prioritize user experience and strive to provide accurate and timely transcriptions.

Thank you for choosing Transcripto, and we hope you enjoy using our service!
